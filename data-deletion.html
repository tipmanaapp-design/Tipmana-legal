Tipmana – Account Deletion

GitHub Pages-ready U.S.-only 18+

You can permanently delete your Tipmana account directly from within the app at any time. Follow the steps below to initiate account deletion.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Sign in to Tipmana from the opening screen.
  2. Once signed in, tap the gear icon (⚙️) in the top-right corner of the app, located next to the refresh button.
  3. An options menu will appear — tap Profile to navigate to your verified profile page.
  4. Scroll to the bottom of your profile page and tap the Delete Account button.
  5. A confirmation prompt will appear with two options:
    • Delete all my reviews — permanently removes your account and all reviews you have submitted.
    • Keep Reviews (Anonymize) — permanently deletes your account but retains your reviews in an anonymized form, with no connection to your identity.
  6. Select your preferred option to confirm. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
⚠️ Please note: Once your account is deleted, all associated personal data will be removed from our systems in accordance with our Privacy Policy. This process may take up to 30 days to fully complete.

How to Delete or Edit an Individual Review

If you'd like to remove or edit a specific review without deleting your entire account, you can do so directly from the business page:

  1. Search for the business where you left the review using the app's search feature.
  2. On the business page, tap the "Jump to My Comment" button — this will scroll directly to your review.
  3. Tap the red trash can icon 🗑️ on your review to delete it, or tap the edit icon to modify it.
  4. Confirm your selection when prompted.
💡 Tip: Deleting an individual review only removes that specific review — your account and all other data remain fully intact.
