This Privacy Policy describes how Tipmana (“Tipmana,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and otherwise processes personal information in connection with our mobile application and related services (collectively, the “Services”).

0. Notice at Collection (Summary)

In short: We collect basic account information and any content you choose to post (like comments). We use that information to provide the Services, keep them safe, and improve them. We may share information with service providers that help us run the app, and as required by law. We do not sell personal information.

Categories of personal information we collect may include:

  • Identifiers (e.g., name, email, account ID)
  • Internet or other electronic network activity information (e.g., app usage, log data)
  • Geolocation data (approximate location if you enable it)
  • User-generated content (e.g., comments you post)

Purposes for collection/use include:

  • Provide and maintain the Services
  • Personalize and improve the Services
  • Communicate with you
  • Security, fraud prevention, and compliance

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

Information you provide to us

  • Account information: such as your name (or display name), email address, and authentication details when you create an account or sign in.
  • User-generated content: such as comments, ratings, or other content you submit through the Services. If you choose to post anonymously, we will display your content without showing your name.
  • Communications: if you contact us, we may collect the information you provide in your message.

Information we collect automatically

  • Device and usage information: such as IP address, device identifiers, app interactions, and crash/diagnostic data.
  • Log information: including time stamps, pages/screens viewed, and actions taken in the app.

Location information

If you enable location permissions, we may collect approximate or precise location information (depending on your device settings) to support location-based functionality, such as finding businesses near you.

Information from third parties

We may receive information from third-party services you use to sign in (for example, Google), such as your name, email address, and profile information, depending on your settings with that service.
