Thank you for using Tipmana ("Tipmana," "we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our mobile application and related services (the "Service").

Tipmana is offered only to users located in the United States and is intended for individuals 18 years of age or older. If you are a California resident, please review Section 6 (California Privacy Notice) for additional disclosures and rights under the CCPA/CPRA. By using the Service, you agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

0. Notice at Collection (Summary)

We collect the categories of information described below to: provide the Service (accounts, map/business search, posting comments), maintain safety and security, debug and improve the app, and show/measure advertising (via Google AdMob). For more details, see Sections 1–6 and 8–9.

Where required, we provide "just-in-time" notices in the app (for example, when requesting location permission).

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in three main ways: (1) information you provide, (2) information collected automatically, and (3) information from third-party services.

1.1 Information You Provide

Account Information

  • Email address (when you sign up or sign in with email/password).
  • Display name or profile name (including what we receive from Google Sign-In).
  • Authentication information (such as credentials managed by our authentication provider). We do not store your plain-text password.
  • Age eligibility confirmation (for example, an in-app checkbox confirming you are 18+), if collected.

Tipping Experiences and Comments

When you submit feedback about a business, we may collect:

  • The business you are commenting on (place/business ID and name).
  • Subject line and comment text.
  • Selected quick tags (e.g., "Didn't ask for tip", "Has hidden fees").
  • Tip level (e.g., "Poor", "Normal", "Great").
  • Optional tip percentage you choose to share.
  • Whether the comment is posted anonymously.
  • Timestamps (e.g., when the comment was created or updated).

Anonymous posting note: Even if you post anonymously, your comment is still linked internally to your account (for example, via your user ID), so we can enforce rules (like one comment per user per business and allowing you to edit or delete your comment).

Communications with Us

If you contact us (support, bug reports, feedback), we may collect your email address, message content, and any other information you choose to provide.

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

Device and Usage Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device information such as device model, operating system, app version, language, and approximate region.
  • Usage data such as timestamps, screens viewed, interactions with features, and crash logs.
  • Identifiers such as Firebase installation IDs or advertising identifiers used for analytics and ads.

Location-Related Information

If you grant location permissions, we may collect and use your approximate or precise location to:

  • Center the map near your current area.
  • Improve search relevance for nearby businesses.

You can disable location permissions in your device settings at any time. If you do, some location-based features may not function properly.

We do not use location data to continuously track you outside of what is needed to provide map/search features, and we do not build a location history for advertising purposes.

1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

We integrate with third-party services that may collect and process information in connection with your use of Tipmana:

Firebase Authentication (Google/Firebase)

Used to authenticate users via email/password, Google Sign-In, or anonymous sign-in. We may receive:

  • Your email address.
  • Your display name.
  • Authentication tokens and unique user identifiers.

Firebase Firestore (Google/Firebase)

Used to store:

  • Account-related data (e.g., user ID, metadata).
  • Tipping experiences and comments you submit.
  • Miscellaneous app data needed for the Service.

Google Places / Maps Platform

Used to:

  • Search for businesses.
  • Fetch business details, including name, address, and latitude/longitude.

Your search queries and related data may be sent to Google to return results.

Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use AdMob to serve banner ads (for example, on the map screen and business profile screen). AdMob and its partners may collect data such as:

  • Device identifiers and advertising ID.
  • Approximate location.
  • App usage and interaction with ads.

This helps AdMob provide and measure ads, which may include personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your device settings and applicable law.

Please review the privacy policies of these providers for further details on their data practices.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for:

Providing and Maintaining the Service

  • Authenticate users and manage logins.
  • Let you search for businesses and view/post tipping experiences.
  • Enforce rules like limiting one comment per user per business.

Improving and Personalizing the Service

  • Analyze how users interact with features to improve design and performance.
  • Debug and fix crashes, bugs, and performance issues.

Communicating with You

  • Send important account-related updates, security alerts, or notices of policy or term changes.
  • Respond to your questions, feedback, and support requests.

Safety, Security, and Compliance

  • Detect, prevent, or address abuse, fraud, or violations of our Terms of Service.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations and law enforcement requests.

Advertising and Monetization

  • Show ads via AdMob to support the Service.
  • Measure ad performance and related metrics.

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share information in the following ways:

3.1 Service Providers and Vendors

We share information with companies that help us operate the Service, such as:

  • Firebase (authentication, database, infrastructure).
  • Google Places / Maps (business search/details).
  • Ad networks like AdMob (to display and measure ads).

These providers process information to provide services to us and are expected to handle it in accordance with applicable laws and their own policies.

3.2 Other Users

When you submit a comment, its content (subject, text, quick tags, tip level, and optional tip percentage) is visible to other users.

If you choose to post anonymously, we do not show your email or real name, though your comment remains internally associated with your account.

3.3 Legal and Safety Reasons

We may disclose information if we believe it is reasonably necessary to:

  • Comply with a law, regulation, legal process, or government request.
  • Enforce our Terms of Service.
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety, or the rights, property, or safety of our users or others.

3.4 Business Transfers

If we undergo a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to applicable confidentiality requirements and laws.

3.5 No Independent Marketing by Us

We do not share your email address with third parties for their own independent marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide and maintain the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Retention by category (general):

  • Account information (email, user IDs, account metadata): retained while your account is active. If you request account deletion, we delete or anonymize personal account information, subject to legal/security retention requirements.
  • Tipping experiences and comments: retained while visible in the app. If you delete a comment, we remove it from public display and delete or anonymize underlying data where feasible, except where we must retain limited records for legal, security, or abuse-prevention purposes.
  • Device/usage data (analytics, crash logs): retained for a limited period appropriate for debugging, security, and improving the Service, then deleted or de-identified.
  • Location data: used to provide map/search features. We do not maintain a continuous location history for tracking; any location data we process is used in connection with the requested feature and is not retained longer than necessary for that purpose.
  • Aggregated/De-Identified Data: We may retain non-identifiable aggregate data (for example, stats about total tip levels for a business) even after your account or comments are deleted, as long as it cannot reasonably be used to identify you.

5. Your Choices and Controls

5.1 Access, Update, and Deletion Controls

You can update certain account information (such as email) using in-app options if available, or by contacting us.

You can edit or delete your own comments directly in the app where this functionality is provided.

5.2 Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account by:

  • Using the in-app account deletion option under the gear icon (top right of the app); or
  • Contact us using the details in the Contact Us section.

When we process an account deletion request, we will:

  • Delete or anonymize personally identifiable account information (such as email), subject to any applicable retention requirements.
  • Remove or anonymize your comments from public display where feasible, or otherwise detach them from your identity so they are no longer linked to you, subject to legal/security exceptions.

5.3 Location Settings

You can control whether Tipmana has access to your device location via your device's settings. Disabling location access may limit some map/search features, but you can still use many parts of the app.

5.4 Advertising & Tracking Controls

Depending on your device/OS, you may be able to:

  • Reset your advertising ID.
  • Limit ad tracking or personalized ads in system settings.
  • AdMob may also honor applicable platform-level privacy/ads settings.

6. California Privacy Notice (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA).

6.1 Categories of Personal Information We Collect

In the last 12 months, we may have collected the following categories (as defined by California law):

Identifiers

  • Email address.
  • User IDs assigned by Firebase or similar systems.
  • Advertising identifiers from your device.

Internet or Other Electronic Network Activity

  • Usage data such as app interactions, screens viewed, timestamps, and crash logs.
  • Interaction with ads.

Geolocation Data

  • Approximate or precise location, if you grant location permission, used for map and business search functionality.

Commercial or User-Generated Content

  • Comments, tip levels, quick tags, and optional tip percentages you submit about businesses.

Inferences

  • We may derive simple internal metrics (for example, count of comments posted), but we do not create detailed profiles about you.

6.2 Sources of Personal Information

We collect personal information from:

  • You directly (account creation, comments).
  • Your device and app usage (automatic collection).
  • Third-party service providers (Firebase, Google Places/Maps, AdMob).

6.3 Purposes for Collecting Personal Information

We use these categories of information for the purposes described in Sections 2–5 above (providing the Service, security, analytics, advertising, etc.).

6.4 Sale or Sharing of Personal Information (California)

We do not sell personal information for money.

However, California law defines "sell" and "share" broadly. Our use of advertising technology (such as AdMob) may involve disclosing certain identifiers and device/usage information to advertising partners to display and measure ads. Depending on how advertising technology is configured and used, this may be considered "sharing" for cross-context behavioral advertising under California law.

Because Tipmana is intended for users 18+, we do not knowingly sell or share the personal information of minors under 16.

6.5 Sensitive Personal Information

If you allow precise geolocation, California law may treat it as Sensitive Personal Information. We use location data only to provide map/search features and do not use it to continuously track you.

6.6 Your California Rights

California residents may have the following rights (subject to certain exceptions):

  • Right to Know / Access: Request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we collected about you.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information we collected from you, subject to legal, security, or operational exceptions.
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal information we maintain about you.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing: You may opt out of "sale" and/or "sharing" as defined by California law.
  • Right to Limit Use/Disclosure of Sensitive Personal Information: You may request that we limit our use/disclosure of Sensitive Personal Information (such as precise geolocation) to what is necessary to provide the Service.

To exercise the rights described above, contact us at:

If we offer in-app privacy controls (for example, a "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" option or similar), you may also use those controls.

  • Confirming information associated with your account (such as email address), and/or
  • Asking you to authenticate while logged into the app.

8. Age Requirement (18+)

Tipmana is not directed to, and is not intended for, individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

If we learn that we have collected personal information from a person under 18, we will take steps to delete that information and may take additional steps to restrict access to the Service.

If you believe someone under 18 has provided personal information to Tipmana, please contact us at the email address in the Contact Us section.

9. U.S.-Only Service

Tipmana is intended for and offered only to users located in the United States. We do not target, market to, or knowingly provide the Service to individuals outside the United States.

Our Service and servers are located in the United States, and we process and store information in the United States.

10. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Using reputable providers (like Firebase and Google Cloud) for authentication and data storage.
  • Applying Firestore security rules that restrict:
  • Creation of comments to authenticated users.
  • Updating and deleting of comments to the user who created them.
  • Limiting access to personal information to personnel and systems that need it.

However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we strive to protect your information as best we can.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We will update the "Last updated" date at the top.
  • We may provide additional notice within the app or via email where required by law.

Your continued use of Tipmana after changes take effect is deemed acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

12. Service Discontinuation

If Tipmana is permanently discontinued, we will take the following steps to protect your data:

  • Advance notice: We will provide at least 30 days advance notice via the app or email where available before the Service is permanently shut down.
  • Data export and deletion requests: During the notice period, users may request deletion of their data using the in-app account deletion feature or by contacting us.
  • Data deletion: Within 90 days of permanent discontinuation, we will delete or anonymize all personally identifiable information, including account information, user IDs, email addresses, and any data that could reasonably be used to identify you.
  • Aggregate data: Non-identifiable aggregate data (such as anonymous tipping statistics) may be retained, as long as it cannot reasonably be used to identify any individual user.

In the event of a temporary suspension of the Service for maintenance or other operational reasons, your data will remain stored securely and will not be deleted. The Service will resume as soon as reasonably possible, and no action is required on your part.
